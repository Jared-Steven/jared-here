Questions People Ask Me

If your question isn't addressed here, feel free to book a call--I'll always be ready to provide further assistance!


What industries do you specialize in? +
I've worked across various sectors including finance, healthcare, e-commerce, and tech startups. My skills are adaptable to any industry that leverages data and technology.
How long does a typical project take? +
Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A simple API integration might take a few weeks, while a full AI model development could span several months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.
Can you work with our existing tech stack? +
Absolutely. I'm proficient in a wide range of technologies and can adapt to your current infrastructure. If beneficial, I can also recommend improvements or alternatives.
Do you offer ongoing support after project completion? +
Yes, I offer post-project support packages tailored to your needs, whether it's maintenance, updates, or staff training.
What's your process for technical writing projects? +
I start with understanding the target audience and goals, conduct thorough research, create an outline, write the content, and then refine it based on your feedback. I ensure the final product is clear, accurate, and engaging.
Do you work remotely or on-site? +
I primarily work remotely, which allows for flexibility and cost-effectiveness. However, I'm open to on-site work for critical project phases or workshops if needed.
How do you price your services? +
I offer flexible pricing models including project-based, hourly, and retainer options. We'll discuss your needs and budget to find the most suitable arrangement.
What's your availability for new projects? +
My availability varies, but I typically book projects 2-4 weeks in advance. For urgent needs, I may be able to accommodate shorter timelines.
How do you ensure clear communication throughout the project? +
I maintain regular check-ins, provide detailed progress reports, and use project management tools to keep all stakeholders informed. I'm always available via email or scheduled calls to address any questions or concerns.
